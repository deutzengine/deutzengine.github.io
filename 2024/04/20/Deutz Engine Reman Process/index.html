Deutz Engine Reman Process 道依茨发动机官方再制造流程

With our DEUTZ engine reman reconditioning process you will receive a DEUTZ reman engine with the highest quality standards. Here you can gain an insight into the individual process steps that guarantee a product that is manufactured with and according to the latest technical know-how on state-of-the-art machines.

经过道依茨发动机工厂的再制造升级，您将获得类似全新道依茨发动机性能的机器。以下是道依茨发动机官方再制造工厂的生产流程介绍。

Disassembly 完全拆解

The used engine is completely disassembled. All wear parts are discarded. All reusable parts are cleaned.

需要维修或者再制造的道依茨发动机会进行完全拆解，缸套、活塞、气门油封、曲轴瓦等易损件均被替换，曲轴、连杆、缸盖、缸体、凸轮轴等可重复使用的部件都会进入喷砂、抛丸、超声波清洗等工序进行清洁。

Inspection 检验

Diagnosis of all parts to be re-used.Decision on reuse,reconditioning or scrapping.Further use of the current series status without exception.Access to an extensive findings database and documentation.

利用磁粉探伤等检测工艺，检查可重复使用的部件，确保合格的旧件用于道依茨发动机再制造装配。

Reconditioning 修复

The engine components are reconditioned according to DEUTZ’s own specifications on modern machinery.

对有公差的零部件，道依茨发动机工厂技术人员利用数控机床和机加工设备进行修复。

Assembly 组装

Assembly is made under series production conditions.Replacement of wear parts by DEUTZ genuine parts.

用道依茨发动机原厂零件更换易损件，连同修复后合格的旧件，在工厂的组装工位，按照道依茨原厂标准进行发动机组装。

Quality check 质检
